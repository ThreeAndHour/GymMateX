Every brand should create two complementary types of AI influencers to cover the consumer journey from awareness to trust.

Recommendations for Advancement

Bridge the Two Avatars

Create interactions between them to make their world feel real.

  • Have the Lookalike learn from and reference the Evangelist.
  • Have the Evangelist use the Lookalike's story as a case study.

Design a Persona Journey

Make the avatars dynamic and allow them to grow with their audience.

  • Evangelist: Always updates with new knowledge and trends.
  • Lookalike: "Grows" from a beginner to an intermediate user/advisor.

This is the shift from broadcasting to nurturing relationships at scale.
