INITIAL PROVOCATION

Cambodia initiated the conflict by planting PMN-2 anti-personnel mines in Thai territory, violating the Ottawa Treaty, and then launching the first military attack.

ROCKET ATTACKS ON CIVILIANS

Cambodian forces fired BM-21 multiple rocket launchers into Thai civilian zones. These war crimes resulted in casualties including women, children, and the elderly, and destroyed shops and homes.

FAKE NEWS & DISINFORMATION

A large-scale disinformation campaign is being waged by Cambodia, using state media and thousands of social media accounts to spread false narratives and paint Thailand as the aggressor.

WEAPONIZING IMAGERY

Images are deliberately twisted. A Reuters photo of a firefighting plane was falsely presented as a chemical attack. Photos of Thai territories, like a burned 7-Eleven, were used to spread false narratives.

POLITICAL GAMES

Cambodia fuels a boycott against Thai products by falsely accusing Thailand of mistreating Cambodian workers. Thailand reaffirms its fair treatment and urges Cambodia to stop harming its own workers for political games.

THAILAND'S STANCE

Thailand has always sought a peaceful resolution, acting only in self-defense and adhering to international law. The message is clear: Stop the attacks. Stop the lies. Demand accountability.
