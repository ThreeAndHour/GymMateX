The Dual-Avatar Strategy: Creating Two-Dimensional Influence

Every brand should create two types of AI influencers that work in tandem to cover the entire consumer journey, from building awareness to establishing trust.

Types of AI Influencers

üß†

1. Category Evangelist

An AI expert in your product category, designed to educate and build credibility.

Key Functions:

  • The Trust Builder
  • Establishes brand/category authority
  • Provides in-depth information, answers logical questions

Example: "Dr. Brown," a virtual nutritionist explaining plant-based proteins.

ü§ù

2. Buyer Lookalike

An AI representing your target customer, designed to reflect their problems and create a sense of relatability.

Key Functions:

  • The Empathy Bridge
  • Reflects target customer's image and pain points
  • Builds "Relatability" and an emotional connection

Example: "Three, a part time fitness trainer" reviewing protein powder recipes.

Recommendations for Further Development

Bridge the Two Avatars

Create interactions between them to make their world feel more real and engaging.

  • Have the Lookalike learn from and reference the Evangelist.
  • Have the Evangelist use the Lookalike's story as a case study.

Design the Persona's Journey

Bring the avatars to life and let them grow with their audience.

  • Evangelist: Always updates with new knowledge and trends.
  • Lookalike: "Grows" from a beginner to an intermediate user/advocate.

This is the shift from mass broadcasting to nurturing relationships at scale.
